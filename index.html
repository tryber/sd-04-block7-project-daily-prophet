PROFESSOR ATTACKS STUDENT

In this Wednesday morning, during the dark magic class, students caught the moment when the teacher attacks one of his students! According to the students, this behavior is recurrent during classes and the punishments range from physical aggressions to spells cast on students! The students also said that they never spoke about the case for fear of something worse happening to them. According to reports by several students in the class, Professor Snape has aggressive and frightening behavior and they do not feel safe with his guidance. Several students have already canceled enrollment in the discipline and several others have already appeared injured, but no explanation has ever been associated with the conduct of the teacher who remains unclear about the case.

When our team approached the professor in order to get some explanation about the case, he simply ignored us, saying that the footage would be a boycott of the students towards him, due to the rigidity applied in the classroom. After the subject went viral throughout the institution, the professor released a note saying that his actions were done in self-defense, since the students were practicing the spells taught by the teacher against himself!
Although no conclusions have been reached regarding the case, the college council decided to suspend Snape's title and place him in temporary exile from his activities. He continues to deny the accusations and promises to come back even better, with even more precious content for his "beloved students", as he says.

"I felt depreciated in every way! Even my wand was threatened! I don't feel safe with Mr Snape around."

NOW, SPORTS!

The Hogwarts team moved on to the final of the Quidditch world championship. Our students are training extensively to try to bring home the first title in the school's history! Will they succeed? The technical team has developed spells and potions to help athletes in training, focusing on their fitness and concentration. The coach said that the strategy remains the same for the entire championship: speed and aggression against the opposing team.

According to him, the best defense is an excellent attack and they do not intend to let these opportunities pass! In 95 years of college, this is the first time that the institution's team has advanced so far in the world championship. At student championships, the Hogwarts team has a collection of 45 trophies, but the ambition of young athletes continues to aim far. Is the world championship of the year ours?
